Zuma

Located in the heart of downtown Dubai, Zuma offers contemporary Japanese cuisine in a sophisticated and vibrant setting. From sushi to robata grill dishes, Zuma provides an exquisite dining experience.

Recommended Dish: Black Cod Miso

Pierchic

Set on a pier extending into the Arabian Gulf, Pierchic is a seafood restaurant with breathtaking views of the Dubai coastline. Enjoy a fine dining experience with a menu featuring the freshest catch of the day.

Recommended Dish: Lobster Thermidor

Al Hadheerah

Transport yourself to an Arabian fantasy at Al Hadheerah, located in Bab Al Shams Desert Resort & Spa. This restaurant offers a traditional Arabian buffet and live entertainment under the stars.

Recommended Dish: Mixed Grill

La Petite Maison

La Petite Maison is a French-Mediterranean restaurant in DIFC, known for its vibrant atmosphere and fresh, seasonal ingredients. The menu is inspired by the culinary diversity of the Mediterranean region.

Recommended Dish: Truffle Risotto
